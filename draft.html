Internet Phone Book

We are creating a physical directory for exploring the vast poetic web. It features 200 designers, developers, writers, curators, and educators and their personal websites.

Open call for sites

We are seeking websites that are personal, poetic, or human in other ways. If you would like your site to be considered of the first edition, please use the form below to submit your website.

Book release

The first edition of the Internet Phone Book will be released at Naive Yearly on Friday, September 20th, 2024 at Museum of Architecture and Design Ljubljana, Slovenia.
